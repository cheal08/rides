San Agustin Church

San Agustin Church, located in Intramuros, Manila, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the oldest churches in the Philippines. Built in 1607 by the Spanish Augustinian friars, it showcases stunning Baroque architecture and has survived numerous earthquakes, fires, and wars. The church remains a powerful symbol of Filipino heritage and resilience.
